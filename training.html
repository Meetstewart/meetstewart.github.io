1

Context is Everything

Tell your VE who you are, what you do, and what matters most. "I'm a sales director at a SaaS company. My biggest priority is closing Q1 deals."

2

Introduce Your People

Let your VE know who's who. "Sarah is my CEO ‚Äî always be formal. Mike is my co-founder ‚Äî casual is fine." This shapes how they draft communications.

3

Share Your Preferences

Tell them how you like things done. "Keep emails under 3 paragraphs. I prefer bullet points. Never schedule meetings before 10am."

4

Correct and Refine

When something isn't quite right, say so. "Too formal ‚Äî make it friendlier" or "Good, but add more detail about the pricing." They learn from every interaction.

5

Build on Success

When they nail something, tell them. "Perfect, save that style for future investor updates." Positive reinforcement helps them understand your standards.
